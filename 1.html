Intro.

IDND: Industrial Design and Data

We provide industrial & engineering data exchange service based on block-chain.

In order to do so, we create ecosystem where creators do not need to worry about their copy-rights and can receive rewards based on their level of contribution.

Furthermore, we provide convenience for 3D printing industry by establishing data exchange that never existed before.

Therefore Makers-Market can evolve from inter-industry data exchange to cross-industry data sharing platform.
